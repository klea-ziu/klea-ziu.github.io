I am a PhD student at **MBZUAI** working on multimodal machine learning and robust AI systems.

My research interests include:
- Vision-language models
- Robust visual recognition and adversarial robustness
- AI for sustainability and clean energy

You can find my publications on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=JLqxspUAAAAJ).

- *MirrorCheck: Efficient Adversarial Attacks via Pruning and Connectivity-Preserving Rewiring on Vision Transformers* (arXiv 2024)
- *DAG: Dynamic Adversarial Grouping for Robust Visual Recognition* (arXiv 2024)
- *A zero-dimensional model for integrating renewable hydrogen into microgrids and remote grids* (Journal of Physics: Energy, 2022)
- *Comparative Life Cycle Assessment of Green and Gray Hydrogen for Fuel Cell Buses in Three European Cities* (2021)
